#### The Mystery of the Puzzle's Riddle

Be the first to solve this puzzle and win some money. How much money? However, the prize pool will **double** for every multiple of 10 subscribers the channel gains from that starting point: $50 at 250 subscribers, $100 at 2500 subscribers, $200 at 25,000 subscribers and so on. The prize money will be awarded to the first person to type the puzzle's answer as a comment on the video. You may work together, but only one may win. Good luck!

### an upside-down puzzle
    
There exists a five-letter word that when written in all-capital letters, can be read the same upright and upside-down. If this word is played in Scrabble on the first turn, centered on the board, the number of points it yields also follows this upside-down rule, but only under a certain condition. Under that condition, this number is also used to refer to a certain type of person. This type of person can also be represented by a symbol. If you turn that symbol upside down, it can have a few different meanings, but one of them in particular was used in Medieval European alchemy to refer to a certain element. This element on the modern periodic table has a two-letter symbol. If you displayed this symbol as best you could on a standard, 7-segment digital clock display and then flipped the clock upside-down, it would then display the shortened nickname of a video game character. This video game character's English voice actor was born in a state whose two-letter postal abbreviation can be written upside-down on a standard, 7-segment digital clock display to make a number. Finally, what is that number minus 2?

After looking up the names and addresses, the agent discovers they are all fake and therefore believes the list to be some sort of coded message. Due to the rest of the address book being blank, the agent believes that the entire message is coded within the following list.
